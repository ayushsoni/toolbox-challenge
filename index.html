Game Rules

    Welcome to the Memory Game
    You get started by hitting the "Start Thinking" button, which sets and resets your timer to 0 seconds.
    Next, you begin by clicking on any of the 16 tiles you see on the page. The objective is to click on another tile that may be hiding a similar image to the one you first clicked.
    If you click on two tiles consecutively with the same image displayed on the other side of it, your "Matches" will increase and your remaining pairs decrease.
    For every tile you click that does not have the same image as the first one, your "Missed" will continue to increase.
    The goal of this game is to get your "Matches" to 8 pairs in the shortest amount of time without increasing the number of "Missed" tiles.
